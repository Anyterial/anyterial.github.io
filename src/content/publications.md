## ADAQ 

Automatic Defect Analysis and Qualification (ADAQ) is a software platform for automatic workflows for high-throughput calculations of point defects in semiconductors.

### Main publication

- [ADAQ: Automatic workflows for magneto-optical properties of point defects in semiconductors](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cpc.2021.108091),<br/>
  J. Davidsson, V. Ivády, R. Armiento, and I. A. Abrikosov, Computer Physics Communications, 108091 (2021).

### Tools

- [ADAQ-SYM: Automated Symmetry Analysis of Defect Orbitals](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.04381),<br/>
  W. Stenlund., J. Davidsson, V. Ivády, R. Armiento, and I. A. Abrikosov, arXiv:2307.04381 [cond-mat.mtrl-sci] (2023).
